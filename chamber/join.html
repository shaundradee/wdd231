Membership Levels

Nonprofit Membership

Nonprofit Membership (Free)

Ideal for local nonprofits and community organizations.

  • Access to general chamber events
  • Listing in the chamber member directory
  • Networking opportunities with local businesses

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership ($)

Great for small businesses starting out.

  • All nonprofit benefits, plus:
  • Discounts on chamber-hosted events
  • Monthly member newsletter inclusion
  • Invitation to basic business workshops

Silver Membership

Silver Membership ($$)

Perfect for growing businesses seeking visibility.

  • All Bronze benefits, plus:
  • Featured spotlight in member directory
  • Social media promotion by the chamber
  • Priority access to training seminars and mixers
  • One free booth at the annual business expo

Gold Membership

Gold Membership ($$$)

Designed for established businesses ready to lead.

  • All Silver benefits, plus:
  • Rotating homepage spotlight on the chamber website
  • Sponsorship opportunities at high-profile events
  • VIP invitations to exclusive networking receptions
  • Customized advertising package
